Microcenter: 32 inch 4k monitor

link

The menu bar is the first place I go to and the following subtitles. I found 4k monitor directly without manually entering anything. I use the filter section on the left to filter 32’’ since I did not see any 32’’ monitor when skimming the options pop out. Since I know little about monitors I sort the results by rating and click into the first product. I check the overview of what this monitor is suitable for and manually check the size compared to my laptop to see if it will fit in my desk. The scrollable page turner feature is very useful since it allows me to see the product in all directions as I’m seeing it in person. After I added it to the cart, insurance and ‘often bought with’ section pop up along with the add confirmation which is very convenient.
